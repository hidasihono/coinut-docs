A trade is the conversion step of an autoramp flow — created once a linked [deposit](deposits) is `CONFIRMED`, and executes the actual currency conversion.

## Status

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `CREATED` | Trade initiated |
| `SETTLED` | Conversion executed |
| `CANCELED` | Trade canceled |

## Fetching Trades

- [`GET /trade/list`](api/trade-list) <Icon icon="link" size={14} /> — paginated, scoped to the authenticated partner, filter by `status`
- [`GET /trade/detail`](api/trade-detail) <Icon icon="link" size={14} /> — single trade by `id`

Each trade includes `fromAmount`/`fromCurrency` and `toAmount`/`toCurrency`, plus one of:

- `deposit` — `{id, customerName}` when the originating deposit used the current deposit flow
- `legacyDeposit` — `{id, customerName}` (numeric `id`) when it used the legacy flow — see [Deposits](deposits)
- `withdrawal` — `{txId}`, present once the outgoing crypto transaction has broadcast (may be absent on early deliveries of `TRADE_SETTLED`)

<Warning>
  For offramp flows, `TRADE_SETTLED` confirms the crypto-to-fiat **trade** executed — it does **not** confirm the bank transfer landed. There is currently no separate webhook for offramp bank-transfer completion. Treat `TRADE_SETTLED` as "funds are queued for payout" and reconcile actual bank arrival through your own banking feed or Coinut support — don't build logic that expects a further webhook. See [Bank Accounts](bank-accounts) for typical settlement windows by currency.
</Warning>

## On-Chain Verification (Onramp)

For onramp trades, once `withdrawal.txId` is present, verify the transaction on-chain:

- ERC-20: `https://etherscan.io/tx/{withdrawal.txId}`
- TRC-20: `https://tronscan.org/#/transaction/{withdrawal.txId}`

## Monitoring

Subscribe to `TRADE_CREATED` and `TRADE_SETTLED` rather than polling. See [Webhooks](webhooks) and [Payload Examples](webhooks-payload-examples).
